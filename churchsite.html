Our Story

Church Point was founded in 2010 with a vision to create a community of believers who support and encourage one another in their faith journey. We believe in the power of God's word to transform lives and communities.

Our mission is to spread the Gospel, make disciples, and serve those in need. We welcome people from all walks of life to join us in worship and fellowship.
