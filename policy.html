Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are important to us

Effective Date: October 28, 2025

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Fun Tech Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") built the Church Point app as a tool for churches to manage their operations, members, and communications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application or any related services.

By using Church Point, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services. This includes:

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Gender and date of birth (if provided by your church)
  • Payment details (through secure third-party processors)
  • Attendance and membership records

Non-Personal Information

  • App usage data and analytics
  • Device information such as model, operating system, and app version

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Manage church members and attendance
  • Facilitate communication between church leaders and members
  • Process tithes, donations, and other payments
  • Send notifications or important church updates
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the app

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to deliver core functionality:

  • Supabase – for user authentication, database, and secure storage
  • Twilio – for sending communication messages (e.g., SMS or WhatsApp notifications)
  • Paynow – for processing secure payments and donations

Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy regarding how they handle user data. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To trusted service providers who assist in operating the app (under confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Data Rights

You may:

  • Access and update your information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account or personal data
  • Opt-out of certain communications

Requests can be made through your church administrator or by contacting us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for use by churches and their registered members. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the app means you accept any modifications.

9. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Church Point account and all associated personal data, you can submit a request through your church administrator or directly to us.

Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and personal data:

  • Contact your church administrator, or
  • Email us directly at: churchpointorg@gmail.com with "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our systems within 30 days of verification.
